Terms & Privacy

Last updated: January 2026

1 Terms and Conditions

By using the CMU Lost & Found portal, you agree to provide truthful information. Reporting items you do not possess or claiming items that are not yours is a violation of the Student Code of Conduct.

Disposal Policy

In accordance with SAO regulations, found items surrendered to the office will be held for a maximum of sixty (60) calendar days. Unclaimed items after this period will be subject to disposal or university donation.

2 Privacy Policy

We collect your University Email, Name, and Student Number to ensure accountability. We also store photos and descriptions of reported items.

  • Data Visibility: Your contact details are hidden from the public gallery and only visible to the SAO Admin.
  • SMS Notifications: By reporting a lost item, you consent to receive automated SMS alerts when a match is found.
  • Data Retention: Records of resolved cases are archived for one academic year for auditing purposes.

For legal inquiries, contact the Office of Student Affairs (SAO).
